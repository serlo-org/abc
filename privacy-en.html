This app uses the services of Google Analytics as provided by Google Inc, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA. Google Analytics uses textfiles commonly referred to as „Cookies“ which are saved on your device and enable the analysis of your user behaviour within the app. The information thus gathered comprises

  • device information and version
  • the operating system in use on your device
  • the version of the app installed on your device
  • the IP-Address

This data is transmitted via and stored on the servers of Google Inc which are situated in the United States of America. The IP-address transmitted by Google Analytics will not be consolidated with any other data that Google may hold on your person. Moreover, we configured Google Analytics to apply the code “anonymizeIP” in order to have your IP curtailed by Google within the European Union or any other signatory of the GDPR prior to its transmission to the USA. Thus, all data collected is being anonymized. Exceptional circumstances may lead to the IP-address being transmitted to a Googler server situated in the USA and curtailed there.

The App uses the “demographic features” service by Google Analytics. This service allows reports which may include statements about the users age, sex and interests. This data is sourced from targeted advertising provided by Google as well as user information gathered by third parties. This data cannot be matched to any individual person..

Google will use the information gathered to provide analyses of your usage of Serlo ABC commissioned by the app owner to facilitate reports of app activity and other services Google provides for the app owner.

Google generally does not make this information available to Third Parties. Only in exceptional circumstances Google may pass on your information to Third Parties, these include (i) the consent of the app owner, (ii) legal obligation or the protection of Third Party rights, the property and/or security of Google, its users or the general public and (iii) the transmission of data to Third Parties to facilitate the proper functioning of Google. When (iii) applies, the Third Parties concerned are obliged to process the data exclusively as instructed, in accordance with this privacy statement and under application of suitable data protection and security measures.

We hold a Data Processing Agreement with Google and fully apply the strict provisions of German Data Protection Legislation to our usage of Google Analytics.
